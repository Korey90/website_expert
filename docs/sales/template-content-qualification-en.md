## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Have
- [ ] Budget ≥ £199/mo (Starter: 4 blog posts or 8 social captions)
- [ ] Has an existing website or social channel where content will be published
- [ ] Identifiable target audience and topic area (not "write about anything")
- [ ] At least one person who can approve content within a reasonable timeframe
- [ ] Language requirement matches Website Expert capability (EN, PL, PT)

### Nice-to-Have
- [ ] Existing blog or content section on website
- [ ] Active SEO strategy or keyword targets to align content with
- [ ] Brand guidelines or tone-of-voice document available
- [ ] Product or service catalogue as source material
- [ ] Content calendar or seasonal promotional plan
- [ ] Running or planning `seo` retainer (strong cross-sell)

### Red Flags
- 🚨 "I just need someone to write one article" — direct to one-off custom quote, not retainer
- 🚨 "I don't need to read it before it goes live — just publish whatever looks good"
- 🚨 "I need content in 6 languages starting immediately"
- 🚨 Highly regulated industry (e.g., SRA-regulated legal advice, FCA-regulated financial advice) without compliance review process in place
- 🚨 "I want content that makes claims about health / legal / financial outcomes" without expert verification

## Scope and Boundaries
**Starter scope (£199/mo):**
4 × SEO-optimised blog posts OR 8 × social media captions per month. Tone-of-voice guidelines included.

**Custom / Growth scope (from £399/mo):**
Mixed packages: blog + social + email copy. Higher volumes. Multi-language (EN + PL or EN + PT). Product description packages for e-commerce.

## Risks and Dependencies
- Without topic input from client, content quality and accuracy may suffer
- Regulated industries require internal compliance sign-off before publishing
- Publishing schedule depends on client approval turnaround; build SLA into brief
- Multi-language content requires separate approval loops — increases timeline

---

## Assumptions
- Client will provide at least a basic topic brief per article / caption set
- Minimum 3-month engagement for measurable content performance (SEO and engagement)
- Content revisions are limited to 2 rounds per piece at Starter tier
