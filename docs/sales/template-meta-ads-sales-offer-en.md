## FAQ

**What ad spend do you recommend for Meta Ads?**
We recommend a minimum of £300/mo ad spend to gather enough data for optimisation. Our management fee starts at £349/mo.

**What is the Meta Pixel and why do I need it?**
The Meta Pixel is a tracking code on your website that records visitor actions — page views, leads, purchases. Without it, you cannot retarget website visitors or measure what your ads are producing.

**What ad formats do you use?**
Carousel, single image, video, Reels, and lead generation forms — selected based on your objectives and audience behaviour.

**Do I need a Facebook Business Page to run Meta Ads?**
Yes. You need an active Facebook Business Page. We can help you set one up if needed.

**How quickly will I see results?**
Campaigns can go live within 5–7 business days. Month 1 is a learning and setup phase. Expect stronger and more consistent results from Month 2 onwards.

**Do you write the ad copy and source creative?**
Yes — copywriting and image/video sourcing are included in the management fee. We submit creative to you for approval before publishing.
