## Business Problem

Most businesses understand they should be publishing content regularly. In practice, it rarely happens — because it takes time, requires specialist writing skill, and demands consistency that internal teams cannot always deliver. Without content, your SEO strategy stalls, your social media goes quiet, and your competitors fill the space you left empty.

## What Is Not Included

- Graphic design, image creation, or video production (sourcing of royalty-free images can be included on request)
- Publishing content to your website or social media platforms (content is delivered ready to publish; scheduling is the client's responsibility unless agreed otherwise)
- SEO keyword research (this should come from an active SEO retainer — we can provide basic keyword targets, but deep research is a separate service)
- Translations of pre-existing content (new content creation only in the agreed language(s) — translation of existing content quoted separately)
- Guaranteed rankings from content alone (content supports SEO but ranking depends on technical SEO, backlinks, and competition)

## FAQ

**How many pieces of content do I get per month?**
Our Starter plan (£199/mo) includes 4 blog posts or 8 social media captions. Custom volumes are available from £399/mo.

**What languages do you write in?**
English, Polish, and Portuguese — written by native-level writers, not machine translations.

**Do you publish the content, or just deliver it?**
We deliver content ready to publish. Unless agreed otherwise, scheduling and publishing is the client's responsibility.

**What if I am not happy with a piece of content?**
One round of revisions is included per piece. We work from your feedback to get it right.

**Do you do keyword research, or do I need to provide keywords?**
For the best results, content should be aligned with a keyword strategy from an SEO retainer. If you do not have an active SEO retainer, we can provide a basic keyword target list to guide content topics.

**Can you write product descriptions for my e-commerce store?**
Yes. Product description writing is available as a custom plan — quoted based on volume and complexity.
