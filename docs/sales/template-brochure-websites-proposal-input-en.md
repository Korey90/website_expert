## Risks and Dependencies
- **Content deadline** — if client misses content deadline by more than 7 days, launch date shifts accordingly
- **Brand assets** — if logo/colours are not provided before design starts, design phase is delayed
- **Third-party approvals** — domain registrar access, existing hosting account must be provided before migration
- **Feedback turnaround** — design approval expected within 5 business days per round; more rounds = extra cost

## Assumptions
- All content (copy, images, brand assets) delivered by the agreed client deadline
- A maximum of 2 design revision rounds is included in the price
- Hosting is not included unless specified (refer to `maintenance` for managed hosting from £29/mo add-on)
- Post-30-day bugs or change requests are billed separately
