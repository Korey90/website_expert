## Ideal Client

This offer is for you if:

- You are a small or medium-sized business, sole trader, startup, or local service provider
- You need a professional online presence but do not need to sell products directly from the website
- Your current website is outdated, slow, or not generating enquiries

## Business Problem

Most businesses lose potential customers before a conversation ever starts. A website that loads slowly, looks unprofessional on mobile, or is difficult to navigate sends visitors straight to your competitors. Without a reliable, fast online presence, you are invisible to people who are actively searching for what you offer.

## Outcome

By the end of this project, you will have:

- A fast, mobile-first website that creates a strong first impression
- A site optimised for search engines from day one — ready to be found in Google
- A simple content management system (CMS) so you can update pages yourself
- A clear path for visitors to contact or enquire with you
- Confidence that your digital presence reflects the quality of your business

## FAQ

**How long does it take to build a brochure website?**
Typically 3–5 weeks depending on the number of pages and how quickly content and feedback are provided.

**Will I be able to update the website myself?**
Yes. We set up a user-friendly CMS so you can edit text, images, and pages without any technical knowledge.

**What is included in the £799 price?**
Up to 5 pages, custom design, CMS, contact form, basic SEO setup, and 30 days of post-launch support.

**What if I need more pages in the future?**
Additional pages can be added after launch. We will quote per page based on complexity and content.

**Do I need to provide a domain and hosting?**
You need a domain. Hosting can be arranged through your own provider, or you can add our managed hosting from £29/mo through a Maintenance plan.

**Can you redesign an existing website rather than build from scratch?**
Yes. We will review your existing site and confirm whether a redesign or rebuild is the right approach.
