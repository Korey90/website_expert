## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Have
- [ ] Budget ≥ £499/mo accepted
- [ ] Existing indexable website (or `brochure-websites` in scope alongside)
- [ ] Realistic timeline expectation: 3–6 months for initial results
- [ ] Willingness to share GA4 + GSC access
- [ ] Minimum 3-month commitment

### Nice-to-Have
- [ ] Active Google Business Profile (accelerates local SEO wins)
- [ ] Existing content or ability to produce new content
- [ ] Interest in bundling `content` service (£199/mo)
- [ ] High lifetime value per customer (SEO ROI is proportional)
- [ ] Competitor already ranking organically (validates SEO demand)

### Red Flags
- 🚨 "I need to be number 1 on Google in 2 weeks"
- 🚨 "Just fix my title tags, that should do it"
- 🚨 "I don't want to pay for analytics setup"
- 🚨 Site is penalised, parked, or has thousands of spam backlinks
- 🚨 Client refused to share GSC data
- 🚨 "Another agency promised me page 1 guaranteed"

---

## Scope and Boundaries
**Minimum viable SEO engagement (£499/mo):**
Technical SEO audit, keyword research, on-page optimisation, monthly GA4 + GSC report, 3-month minimum.

**Scope that triggers re-qualification or tier upgrade:**
- Competitor domain authority significantly higher → need Growth retainer (£799/mo)
- No content production capacity → must add `content` service
- Local SEO priority → confirm Google Business Profile ownership
- E-commerce SEO → product page and schema optimisation adds complexity

## Risks and Dependencies
- Slow site = slow results; technical issues must be resolved first (month 1)
- No content production → on-page optimisation alone has limited ceiling
- Competitive niche → may need Growth retainer to see meaningful progress
- Penalty history → disavow campaign adds 2–3 months before progress is visible

---

## Assumptions
- Lead understands SEO is a long-term investment
- Site CMS is accessible for on-page edits
- Analytics (GA4 + GSC) will be shared before onboarding
