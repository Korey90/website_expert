## Business Problem

A slow or insecure website costs you in two ways: it drives visitors away before they convert, and it pushes your rankings down in Google. Security vulnerabilities — many of which are invisible to the naked eye — expose your business, your customers, and your reputation to real risk. Most websites accumulate technical debt silently until something breaks or is exploited.

## FAQ

**How long does an audit take?**
A full security and performance audit typically takes 5–7 business days. You receive a PDF report and a 1-hour debrief call.

**Do you fix the issues, or just report them?**
The audit produces a full findings report and prioritised fix list. Implementation of fixes can be quoted separately after the audit.

**Is this a penetration test?**
This is a structured security scan and manual review covering OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities, not a full penetration test. If you need a formal pen test, we can advise on the appropriate scope.

**What CMS platforms do you audit?**
We audit WordPress (WooCommerce, plugins), Laravel, custom PHP applications, and other common web stacks.

**Will I understand the report if I am not technical?**
Yes. The report includes a non-technical executive summary alongside the detailed technical findings. The debrief call is specifically designed to explain everything clearly.

**What happens after the audit?**
You receive the report, we debrief, and you decide what to fix and when. We can quote for implementing fixes, or you can take the report to your own developer.
