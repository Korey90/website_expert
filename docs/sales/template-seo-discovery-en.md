## Offer Fit
SEO from Website Expert suits businesses that:
- Have an existing website (or are launching one) and want sustainable organic growth
- Understand SEO takes 3–12 months to show meaningful results
- Have a content strategy need (blogs, service pages, landing pages)
- Want technical SEO, on-page, and link-building handled in one retainer

**Not a fit if:** client needs instant traffic (→ `google-ads` or `meta-ads`). If site does not yet exist, recommend pairing with `brochure-websites` first. If client needs a one-off technical fix only, offer `audits` (£299) as an entry point.

## Discovery Flow

### Current organic performance
> "Are you currently ranking for any keywords? Do you know your traffic numbers?"
> "Do you have Google Search Console connected? Can you share access?"
> "Have you done any SEO before — internally or with another agency?"

### Competitive landscape
> "Who are your main online competitors? Do you know if they rank above you?"
> "Is there a specific competitor you want to overtake?"

### Target keywords and geography
> "What services or products do people search for when they need what you offer?"
> "Is this local (city / county) or national?"
> "Are you targeting a specific language market — English only, bilingual?"

### Technical context
> "When was the site last updated? Is it on WordPress, a custom build, or something else?"
> "Have you had any manual Google penalties or traffic drops in the past 12 months?"
> "How fast is your site? (We can check this for you)"

### Content capacity
> "Are you able to produce new content — blog posts, service pages — or do you need us to write it?"
> "Do you have a content calendar or topic list?"

### Goals and timeline
> "What does success look like in 12 months? First-page ranking? Doubling organic traffic?"
> "Is there a specific season or product launch that SEO needs to support?"

### Budget
> "SEO retainers start from £499/mo. This covers strategy, on-page optimisation, monthly reporting, and initial technical fixes. Does that align with your planning?"

## Risks and Dependencies
- **Slow initial results** — client may not see ranking movement for 3–6 months; must be communicated upfront
- **No GSC / GA4 access** — cannot measure baseline or results without analytics setup
- **Site technical issues** — if site has major speed/indexability problems, first month is technical-fix-heavy
- **Content dependency** — on-page SEO is limited without new content; pairs best with `content` service
- **Domain history** — penalised or spammy backlink profile may require a disavow campaign before gains are visible
- **Competitor investment** — high-DA competitors with large content teams will slow ranking progress in competitive niches
