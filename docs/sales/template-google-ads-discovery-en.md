## Offer Fit
Google Ads management from Website Expert suits businesses that:
- Need immediate, intent-based traffic (as opposed to long-term SEO)
- Have a website with a clear conversion action (form, phone call, purchase)
- Are willing to invest a minimum of £500/mo in ad spend (separate from management fee)
- Want search, shopping, or Performance Max campaigns professionally managed with conversion tracking

**Not a fit if:** client has no website (→ pair with `brochure-websites` first). If client wants social media reach rather than search intent (→ `meta-ads`). If client cannot commit to minimum ad spend.

## Discovery Flow

### Business and product
> "What are you advertising — a product, service, or both? What's the average transaction value?"
> "What does a new customer mean in revenue terms? (LTV or first-sale value)"
> "What is your target cost-per-lead or cost-per-acquisition?"

### Current advertising
> "Are you currently running Google Ads? What's working and what isn't?"
> "Do you have conversion tracking in place — form submissions, calls, purchases tracked?"
> "Has a previous agency managed your account? What happened?"

### Competitors
> "Who are your main competitors? Are they actively advertising on Google?"
> "Have you used the Google Ads preview tool to check who's bidding on your terms?"

### Audience and geography
> "Who is your ideal customer — age, job, location, intent?"
> "Are you targeting a specific city, region, or all of UK?"

### Landing pages
> "Where does the ad traffic land — homepage, a specific service page, or a dedicated landing page?"
> "Is the landing page conversion-optimised? (We can review this)"

### Budget and expectations
> "What's your planned monthly ad budget? (We recommend minimum £500/mo ad spend)"
> "Our management fee starts at £399/mo — that's separate from ad spend. Is that aligned with your planning?"
> "What KPIs matter most to you — leads, ROAS, CPA, impressions?"

## Scope and Boundaries
**In scope (from £399/mo management fee + £500/mo ad spend minimum):**
- Google Search, Shopping, and/or Performance Max campaigns
- Conversion tracking + Google Tag Manager setup
- Keyword research and negative keyword management
- Ad copy writing and A/B testing
- Monthly performance reports with ROI breakdown
- Remarketing and audience targeting

**Out of scope:**
- Meta / Facebook / Instagram ads (→ `meta-ads`)
- SEO or organic growth (→ `seo`)
- Ad creative design (display / video) — available as add-on
- Landing page development (→ `brochure-websites`)
- YouTube / video ad production

## Risks and Dependencies
- **No conversion tracking** — cannot optimise campaigns or prove ROI without tracking in place; must be set up in month 1
- **Weak landing page** — ad traffic converting at <1% suggests a landing page issue, not an ads issue; may need `brochure-websites` alongside
- **Low ad spend** — budgets below £500/mo often do not gather enough data for meaningful optimisation
- **Seasonal volatility** — CPCs spike during peak seasons (Christmas, Black Friday); budget planning must account for this
- **Account history** — taking over a poorly managed account may require a restructure before performance improves
