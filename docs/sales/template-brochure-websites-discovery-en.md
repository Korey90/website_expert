## Offer Fit
A brochure website from Website Expert suits businesses that:
- Need a professional online presence to generate leads or phone enquiries
- Have 1–20 pages of content (or need help creating it)
- Want a fast, mobile-first site without a custom back-end
- Need a CMS to manage their own content going forward

**Not a fit if:** client needs e-commerce, user accounts, bookings with payment, or a SaaS product → escalate to `ecommerce` or `web-applications`.

## Discovery Flow

### Business
> "What does your business do and who is your ideal customer?"
> "How do people currently find you — word of mouth, Google, referrals?"
> "What's the single thing you most want someone to do when they land on your site?"

### Current site pain points
> "What's wrong with your existing website? Or why are you launching one now?"
> "Have you lost work or enquiries because your site doesn't look professional?"

### Goals & success metrics
> "In 6 months, how would you know the new site is working?"
> "Are you expecting calls, form submissions, foot traffic, or online sales?"

### Content & assets
> "Do you have existing copy, photos, and brand assets ready?"
> "Do you need us to write the copy / source images?"
> "How many pages do you think you need?"

### Timeline & urgency
> "Is there a hard deadline — product launch, event, campaign?"
> "Who needs to approve the final design?"

### Budget
> "Projects like this typically run between £799 and £2,500+ depending on scope. Does that range work for you?"

## Scope and Boundaries
**In scope (starter £799):**
- Up to 5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact + 1 more)
- Custom design
- CMS included (editable text, images, pages)
- Contact form with spam protection
- Basic SEO setup (meta titles, descriptions, sitemap, robots.txt)
- SSL & secure hosting setup
- 30 days post-launch support

**Out of scope (requires separate quote):**
- E-commerce / payments
- Booking systems
- Custom user accounts
- More than 5 pages → additional page pricing applies
- Professional photography / videography
- Copywriting (available as `content` add-on from £199/mo)
- Ongoing SEO → `seo` service from £499/mo
- Ongoing maintenance → `maintenance` from £149/mo

## Risks and Dependencies
- **Content delay** — the most common cause of missed launch dates; confirm if client provides or we write copy
- **Feedback bottlenecks** — multiple stakeholders with unclear sign-off process
- **Brand not finalised** — no logo, brand colours, or guidelines available
- **Hosting / domain ownership** — client must own or control domain and hosting account
- **Scope creep** — "just one more page" requests post-sign-off

---

## Assumptions
- Client has an active business with a clear service or product to promote
- Site is informational / lead-capture only (no payments or user logins)
- English is the primary language of the site
- Client will provide domain access and any required third-party credentials
