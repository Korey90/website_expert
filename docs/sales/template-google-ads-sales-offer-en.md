## Business Problem

Running Google Ads without expert management wastes money. Broad keyword targeting, missing negative keywords, untested ad copy, and no conversion tracking mean your budget is being spent — but you have no idea what it is producing. Poor campaign structure drives up cost-per-click and cost-per-acquisition while delivering weak results.

---

## Outcome

With professionally managed Google Ads, you can expect:

- Immediate visibility at the top of Google search results for your target keywords
- Qualified traffic to your website from people actively searching for your product or service
- Conversion tracking in place so every enquiry and sale is tied back to the campaign
- A clear monthly report showing spend, clicks, conversions, and ROI
- Continuously improving performance as campaigns are optimised over time

## FAQ

**What budget do I need for Google Ads?**
We recommend a minimum ad spend of £500/mo. Our management fee starts at £399/mo and is separate from ad spend.

**Can you manage an existing Google Ads account?**
Yes. We start with a free audit of your existing account and identify quick wins before taking over management.

**How quickly will I see results?**
Campaigns can go live within 5–7 business days. Month 1 is a learning phase. Expect improving results from Month 2–3 as campaigns are optimised.

**Do I pay you the ad spend, or Google directly?**
You pay Google directly. Our management fee is separate and covers strategy, setup, optimisation, and reporting.

**What campaign types do you manage?**
Google Search, Shopping (for e-commerce), and Performance Max. Display, YouTube, and Demand Gen are available as add-ons.

**Can I set a daily or monthly budget cap?**
Yes. Budget caps are set at the campaign level within Google Ads. You will never spend more than the limit you set.
