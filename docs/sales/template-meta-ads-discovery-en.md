## Offer Fit
Meta Ads management from Website Expert fits businesses that:
- Have a product or service suitable for social media discovery (not purely intent-based)
- Can supply or approve creative assets (images, video, copy)
- Are ready to invest minimum £300/mo in ad spend (separate from management fee)
- Want to reach custom, lookalike, or interest-based audiences across Facebook and Instagram

**Poor fit when:** client needs purely intent-based traffic (→ `google-ads` instead). No product/visual assets available. Budget below £300/mo ad spend.

## Discovery Flow

### Business and Product
> "What are you selling — product, service, or both? What is the average order or lead value?"
> "Who is your ideal customer — age, lifestyle, location, interests, pain points?"
> "What makes you different from competitors appearing in the same feeds?"

### Current Social Presence
> "Do you have an active Facebook Page and Instagram account?"
> "Is the Meta Pixel installed on your website? Are any custom events firing?"
> "Have you run Meta Ads before? What worked, what didn't?"

### Goals and KPIs
> "Is the primary goal leads, direct sales, brand awareness, or retargeting existing visitors?"
> "What does a successful campaign look like to you — cost per lead, ROAS, reach?"
> "What timeframe are you expecting to see meaningful results?"

### Creative Assets
> "Do you have brand photography, video, or product imagery we can use?"
> "Are you comfortable approving ad copy and creative before we publish?"
> "Do you have any seasonal promotions, offers, or product launches coming up?"

### Landing Pages and Funnel
> "Where does ad traffic land — homepage, product page, or a dedicated landing page?"
> "Is there a clear conversion action — form, checkout, phone call?"
> "Do you run a retargeting audience from website visitors?"

### Budget
> "What is your planned monthly ad spend? (Minimum £300/mo recommended)"
> "Our management fee starts from £349/mo — this is separate from ad spend. Does that fit your budget?"
> "Are you also considering Google Ads? We can run both with a coordinated strategy."

## Scope and Boundaries
**In scope (from £349/mo management + £300/mo minimum ad spend):**
- Facebook and Instagram campaign setup and management
- Meta Pixel and Conversions API setup
- Custom, lookalike, and interest audience targeting
- Ad copy creation and creative direction (image/video sourcing or client-supplied assets)
- Carousel, static image, video, Reels, and Lead Form ad formats
- Retargeting campaigns for website visitors
- Monthly performance reports: ROAS + CPA metrics

**Out of scope:**
- Google Ads or paid search campaigns (→ `google-ads`)
- Organic social media management or posting
- Full video production (minor editing included; full production is extra)
- Website or landing page builds (→ `brochure-websites`)
- Email marketing campaigns

## Risks and Dependencies
- **No Meta Pixel** — without conversion tracking, campaign optimisation is limited to top-of-funnel metrics only; must be set up before launch
- **No creative assets** — Meta campaigns rely heavily on visuals; poor creative = poor results regardless of budget
- **Narrow audience** — highly niche B2B audiences perform poorly on Meta; `google-ads` may be a better fit for intent-driven B2B
- **iOS 14+ limitations** — Apple's privacy changes have reduced Meta's attribution accuracy; clients must understand data may be under-reported
- **Policy restrictions** — certain industries (housing, finance, employment) face Meta's Special Ad Categories with limited targeting
