| Page count | 1–15 pages | 20+ with complex requirements |

## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Have (deal-breakers if missing)
- [ ] Budget ≥ £799 (or realistic expectation of that range)
- [ ] No e-commerce or user authentication requirement
- [ ] Single decision-maker identified or clear approval chain
- [ ] Realistic timeline (minimum 3 weeks from sign-off)

### Nice-to-Have (increase deal quality)
- [ ] Existing brand assets (logo, colours, fonts)
- [ ] Prepared or funded content (copy + images)
- [ ] Interest in maintenance plan (`maintenance`, £149/mo)
- [ ] Interest in SEO retainer (`seo`, £499/mo)
- [ ] Previous failed website project (creates urgency)

### Red Flags
- 🚨 "I want it done in 3 days"
- 🚨 "I'll pay when the site starts making money"
- 🚨 "I need a full e-commerce store for £800"
- 🚨 "I need 50 pages and animated custom effects"
- 🚨 Client has no idea who their customers are
- 🚨 Multiple stakeholders with no agreed brief

---

## Scope and Boundaries
**Minimum viable scope (£799):** 5 pages, custom design, CMS, contact form, basic SEO, 30-day support.

**Scope that triggers re-qualification:**
- 10+ pages → reassess timeline and price
- Copywriting needed → add `content` service or increase project budget
- Blog / news section → budget for CMS training or author onboarding

---

## Risks and Dependencies
- No confirmed budget → quote may be rejected; anchor to value before revealing price
- Decision by committee → risk of indefinite delays without a project champion
- No existing content → timeline will slip; must factor in content production phase

---

## Assumptions
- Lead has been captured via website form, referral, or cold outreach
- Initial qualification happens in a 15–20 min discovery call or email exchange
- If content is missing, it is treated as a separate scope item or referred to `content` service

---

## Open Questions
- [ ] Has the client had a website before? What happened to it?
- [ ] Is there an existing site we're replacing or building from scratch?
- [ ] Who else is involved in the decision?
- [ ] Is there a live campaign, event, or deadline driving urgency?
- [ ] Has the client had quotes from other agencies?
