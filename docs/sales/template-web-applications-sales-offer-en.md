## Ideal Client

This offer is for you if:

- Off-the-shelf software does not fully cover your business needs
- You need a custom web application — a customer portal, SaaS platform, internal tool, or booking system
- You have a complex workflow or process that needs to be automated or digitised
- You want a secure, scalable, and maintainable system built on a professional stack

## Business Problem

Generic software forces your business to adapt to the tool rather than the other way around. You end up paying for features you do not need, missing features you do, and working around limitations that slow down your team and frustrate your customers. Building bespoke — when the scope is right — gives you exactly what you need, built to scale, and fully owned by you.

---

## Outcome

By the end of this project, you will have:

- A custom web application built precisely to your business requirements
- A secure, role-based system where different users see only what they need
- A maintainable codebase with automated tests — easy to extend in the future
- APIs or integrations with the third-party systems your business already uses
- Full documentation and a handover so your team can manage and grow the system independently

## FAQ

**How do you estimate the cost of a web application?**
We run a paid discovery phase (from £599) to define scope, architecture, and a fixed-price quote. The discovery fee is credited towards the build if you proceed.

**Can you take over an existing application?**
Yes. We conduct a code audit first, then propose a takeover or refactor plan depending on the current state of the codebase.

**Why is there a paid discovery phase?**
Web applications are complex. Without a proper discovery phase, any quote would be a guess. The discovery phase protects both sides — you get a realistic scope and price; we get a clear brief. The fee is credited if the project proceeds.

**What technology do you use?**
We use Laravel 13 (backend) and React with TypeScript (frontend) — a proven, scalable stack used by leading organisations worldwide.

**Who owns the code after delivery?**
You do. All source code is yours on final payment.

**Do you offer ongoing development or a retainer after the project?**
Yes. Ongoing development retainers are available and quoted separately once the initial project is delivered.
