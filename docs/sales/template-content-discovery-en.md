## Offer Fit
Content Creation retainer from Website Expert fits businesses that:
- Need consistent SEO-optimised blog articles, landing page copy, or social media captions
- Lack in-house writing resource or need to scale content output without hiring
- Want tone-of-voice guidelines and a structured content calendar
- Are running (or plan to run) SEO or paid ads campaigns that benefit from supporting content

**Poor fit when:** client only needs a one-off piece (single copywriting project → custom quote). Client requires highly technical or regulated content (legal, medical) without subject-matter expert input.

## Discovery Flow

### Business and Goals
> "What is the primary goal of your content — SEO rankings, social engagement, lead generation, or brand credibility?"
> "Who reads your content — existing customers, prospects, industry peers?"
> "Do you have a content strategy or topic framework already, or do we start from scratch?"

### Current Content
> "How often do you currently publish? What types of content — blog, social, email, product descriptions?"
> "What content performs best for you right now, even if it's sporadic?"
> "Is there a backlog of unpublished content or draft materials we can work with?"

### Channels and Format
> "Which channels are most important — website blog, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, email newsletter?"
> "Do you need long-form articles (1,000+ words), short social captions, or both?"
> "Do you have product descriptions that need refreshing or writing from scratch?"

### Brand Voice and Guidelines
> "How would you describe your brand tone — professional, conversational, technical, friendly?"
> "Do you have brand guidelines, a style guide, or examples of content you love?"
> "Are there topics, words, or angles to avoid in your content?"

### SEO and Keywords
> "Are you targeting specific keywords or topics for search rankings?"
> "Do you have a current SEO strategy we should align content to? (Works well with our SEO retainer)"
> "Are there competitors whose content you want to outrank or match?"

### Approval and Workflow
> "Who approves content before it goes live — you, a marketing manager, legal?"
> "How many revision rounds are acceptable per piece?"
> "Do you want us to publish directly to your CMS, or deliver drafts for internal review?"

### Budget
> "Our Starter plan is £199/mo for 4 blog posts or 8 social captions — does that meet your minimum output needs?"
> "Do you need content in multiple languages? (We write in English, Polish, and Portuguese)"

## Scope and Boundaries
**In scope (from £199/mo Starter):**
- SEO-optimised blog articles (keyword research, H-tags, meta descriptions)
- Landing page and service page copy
- Social media captions (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Product descriptions (e-commerce)
- Email newsletter copy and drip sequences
- Tone-of-voice guidelines (included on sign-up)

**Out of scope:**
- Graphic design and social media image creation (→ discuss add-on or `meta-ads` creative)
- Video scripting or production (→ separate quote)
- Press releases or PR copy (→ separate quote)
- Full SEO technical setup (→ `seo` retainer)
- Publishing / scheduling on social platforms (organic social management)

## Risks and Dependencies
- **Topic sourcing** — high-quality content requires client to provide subject-matter expertise (FAQs, case studies, product info); brief client on intake requirements
- **Approval delays** — slow approval cycles delay publishing; agree a clear approval window (e.g., 3 working days)
- **No publishing platform** — if client has no CMS or blog, discuss `brochure-websites` first
- **Regulated industries** — legal, medical, or financial content requires client review before publishing; add compliance note to brief
- **SEO dependency** — content alone without technical SEO foundations may underperform in search; pair with `seo` retainer for best results
