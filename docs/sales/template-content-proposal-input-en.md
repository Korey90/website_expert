## Risks and Dependencies
- **Topic input delays** — if client cannot supply topic briefs on time, content pipeline stalls; agree intake deadline (e.g., 5th of each month)
- **Approval delays** — slow approval extends publishing timeline; SLA must be agreed in writing
- **Regulated content** — any claims about health, finance, or legal outcomes must be reviewed and approved by the client before publishing
- **SEO performance** — blog content alone without technical SEO may not rank; pair with `seo` retainer for full benefit
- **Brand voice alignment** — first 1–2 pieces may need more revision rounds while tone-of-voice is being calibrated
