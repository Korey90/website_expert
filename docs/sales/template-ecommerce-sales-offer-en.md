## Ideal Client

This offer is for you if:

- You sell physical or digital products and want to take orders online
- You are launching a new online store or migrating from an existing platform
- Your current store is slow, hard to manage, or losing sales at checkout
- You need payment processing, inventory management, and order fulfilment integrated in one place
- You want a store that is optimised for conversion, not just a catalogue of products

---

## Business Problem

An online store that loads slowly, has a confusing checkout process, or lacks proper payment integration drives customers away at the final step. Many businesses also struggle with platforms that are expensive, inflexible, or require a developer for every small change — all of which eat into profit margins and slow down growth.

---

## Outcome

By the end of this project, you will have:

- A fast, secure online store ready to take orders and process payments immediately after launch
- A store built around your product catalogue, pricing model, and customer journey
- Payment integrations (Stripe and PayPal) live and tested
- A backend you can manage yourself — add products, update stock, process orders — without developer support
- A foundation ready for future growth: additional products, currencies, and marketing integrations

## FAQ

**WooCommerce or custom — which is better for me?**
WooCommerce suits most SME stores. Custom headless is recommended for 1,000+ SKUs, complex pricing, or when performance is critical.

**Do you handle product data migration from my existing store?**
Yes. We migrate products, categories, customer data, and order history from your existing platform.

**Can I manage the store myself after launch?**
Yes. We configure the CMS so you can add products, update stock, process orders, and run promotions without developer support.

**What payment methods will customers be able to use?**
Stripe and PayPal are integrated as standard. Additional payment methods can be discussed during discovery.

**Will the store work on mobile?**
Yes. All stores are built mobile-first and tested on major devices and browsers before launch.

**Do you offer ongoing support after launch?**
30 days of post-launch support is included. For ongoing updates, monitoring, and security — see our Website Maintenance plan from £149/mo.
