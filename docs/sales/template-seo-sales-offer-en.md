## Ideal Client

This offer is for you if:

- You want to grow organic (unpaid) search traffic month on month
- You are investing in Google Ads or Meta Ads but want to reduce your dependence on paid spend over time
- Your website is not appearing for relevant search terms your customers are using

## Business Problem

Most websites are invisible in search. Poor technical foundations, missing keyword targeting, thin content, and weak authority mean that potential customers never find you — even when they are actively searching for exactly what you offer. Paid ads stop the moment you stop spending. SEO builds an asset that keeps generating traffic.

---

## Outcome

With ongoing SEO and digital marketing, you can expect:

- Measurable growth in organic search traffic month on month
- Higher rankings for keywords your customers are actually searching
- A technically clean website that search engines trust and reward
- Monthly reporting that ties SEO activity directly to business enquiries and revenue
- A reduced cost of customer acquisition over time as organic traffic grows

## FAQ

**How long before I see results from SEO?**
Most clients see measurable improvements in 3–6 months. Competitive niches may take 6–12 months for top-3 rankings.

**Do you offer one-off SEO audits?**
Yes. A standalone technical SEO audit starts from £299 and includes a prioritised fix list and a 1-hour consultation.

**Can you guarantee first page rankings?**
No ethical SEO provider can guarantee specific rankings — anyone who does is misleading you. We commit to best-practice execution, transparency, and measurable monthly progress.

**What access do you need?**
GA4, Google Search Console, and CMS access. We do not need your Google Ads account unless you add that service.

**What is the minimum contract length?**
We recommend a minimum of 6 months to see meaningful results. Contract terms are confirmed at the proposal stage.

**Will you write the content, or do I need to provide it?**
Content writing can be included as an add-on from £199/mo. Alternatively, you can provide your own content and we optimise it.
