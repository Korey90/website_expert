## Business Problem

A website is not a one-off project. Plugins go out of date and create security vulnerabilities. Core software updates can break things if applied without testing. Websites go down — and when they do, every minute costs you customers and revenue. Without regular backups, a single incident can mean losing everything. Most businesses only find out their website has a problem when a customer tells them.

---

## Outcome

With an ongoing Website Maintenance plan, you will have:

- A website that is always up to date — plugins, themes, and core software updated and tested
- 24/7 uptime monitoring so you (and we) know the moment anything goes wrong
- Weekly off-site backups with 30-day retention — your data is always recoverable
- Priority bug fixing with a 2-hour response SLA for critical issues
- SSL certificate renewal managed and monitored — no expired certificates
- A monthly security and performance report so you always know the state of your site

## FAQ

**What happens if something breaks outside office hours?**
Our uptime monitoring alerts us immediately. Critical fixes are addressed within 2 hours on our plan, 24/7.

**Is hosting included in the maintenance plan?**
Managed hosting can be added from £29/mo. We support all major hosts and can migrate your site if needed.

**What CMS platforms do you support?**
WordPress (including WooCommerce), and other standard CMS platforms. Custom Laravel applications are also supported — confirm at enquiry.

**How are updates applied safely?**
We test updates on a staging environment before applying them to your live site. If an update causes a conflict, we roll back and resolve it before proceeding.

**What is included in the monthly report?**
Uptime statistics, backup log, update history, SSL status, and a security and performance summary.

**What if I need more than bug fixing — new pages, features, or a redesign?**
New development work is quoted separately. We can handle it as a one-off project or under a dedicated development retainer.
